## What Happens

1. **Creates keystore** (you'll enter passwords)
2. **Creates key.properties** (you'll enter passwords)
3. **Rebuilds app bundle** (automatically)
4. **Verifies everything** (automatically)

**Total time: ~5 minutes**

