## Privacy Policy for Congé Calculateur

**Last Updated:** [Date]

### Introduction

Congé Calculateur ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

### Information We Collect

#### Data You Provide
- **Vacation Calculations**: Start dates, number of days, and calculation results are stored locally on your device
- **Settings**: Your annual leave days, work schedule preferences, and language selection

#### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data**: App features you use, calculation history (stored locally)

#### Third-Party Services
- **Calendarific API**: We use this service to fetch public holiday information. No personal data is sent to this service.

### How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide vacation calculation services
- Store your calculation history locally on your device
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Fetch accurate public holiday data

### Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: All your data (calculations, settings, history) is stored locally on your device using secure local storage
- **No Cloud Storage**: We do not store your data on external servers
- **No Account Required**: You can use the app without creating an account

### Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your data with third parties for marketing purposes
- Upload your calculations to external servers

We only share:
- Public holiday requests with Calendarific API (no personal data included)

### Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your locally stored data
- Delete your calculation history (via app settings)
- Uninstall the app to remove all local data

### Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us.

Both Google Play and Apple App Store require:
- ✅ What data you collect
- ✅ How you use the data
- ✅ Data sharing practices
- ✅ User rights
- ✅ Contact information
- ✅ Last updated date
